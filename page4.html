SZm41m (OM-14)

SZm41M is Polish gas mask produced under a Russian license of лелю-41 gas mask.
It was the basic gas mask in the Polish army. Just like in the MC-1 mask, it has inhalation valves directed at the visors, thanks to which the visors evaporate when you inhale.
The mask is connected to the combined filter using a rubber pipe, and the combined filter is carried in a bag
The mask also comes with anti-sweat inserts that, when mounted on the visor, do not evaporate, thanks to which visibility increases
These masks also had various bags, and larger quantities were carried in special boxes

All of SZm41 family were produced in 4 sizes ([0,1,2,3] 0 is smallest 3 is biggest)

The SZm41M KF mask is a development version of the SZm41m mask and differs from it in the voice chamber, thanks to which you can hear what the wearer is saying better (As this is the only difference, im give only a few photos)

SZm41

SZm41 is an older version of the SZm41M gas mask.
From newer form it differs in inhalation and exhalation valve as well as tube and bag. this form of mask was produced until the 1960s
