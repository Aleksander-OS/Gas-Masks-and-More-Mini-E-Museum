VM-37

The VM-37 gas mask was the successor of the VM-40 mask, the only difference being that the VM-40 mask is a face mask while the VM-37 is a Hood mask. The reason for this change is that hood masks are more expensive to produce than face masks. By far the most distinctive feature of the mask is its nose-shaped exhalation valve. It works as shown in photo 29. The masks were produced in 4 sizes K, F, M, MU (Kinder, Frauen, Maenner, Maenner Uebergr).
