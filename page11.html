M65

The M65 is a German gas mask once used by the Bunderswehre. Production began in the 1960s and ended around the 1990s. From other masks from this period, it is definitely distinguished by its modernist appearance, which even today stands out from modern masks. By far the most distinctive features of this mask are the triangular visors and the auxiliary half mask inside. The mask is made of very flexible rubber. A definite advantage is the desirable waterproof bag, made of quite hard rubber, well protects the mass from getting wet or damaged.


I personally consider it the most comfortable mask in my collection
