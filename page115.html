"Dzień pasjonatów 2022"

Translated into English, "7th Day of Enthusiasts" was the first event ever where I exhibited my collection. Enthusiasts' Day is an annual event at my primary school where students can set up stands to present their collections or passions in general. Coming back, it was the first time I exhibited my collection, as you can see it wasn't that big back then. This event took place on May 13, 2022 and I remember it very fondly.

The second time I exhibited was on the 8th day of enthusiasts on May 12, 2023, where, in a vote in which students chose the most interesting stands, I won third place and received a diploma.

The next event at which I showed up with my collection was "Ostrowski Dzień Kolekcjonera" (translating into English "Ostrowski's Collector's Day".
